The Vinvel Journey

From shortlisting to long-term care

Hands-on support from choosing the right unit to servicing it with trusted partners.

1. We shortlist the right car

Together we analyse your requirement, market prices and risk, then propose the best auction units or in-stock cars.

2. Inspect in Japan or in Sri Lanka

For auction cars we study sheets, photos and videos; for cars already in Sri Lanka we give you quality time with the car to inspect it properly.

3. Finance, leasing and speed drafts

We connect you with our trusted banking and leasing partners for LC, TT, leasing or speed drafts that match your deal.

4. Bid, ship or hand over keys

Once LC/TT or full payment is confirmed we place the bid and ship the car, or if it is already in Sri Lanka we transfer the keys and documentation.

5. Insurance and protection

We introduce you to insurance partners who understand Japanese imports and can issue cover quickly and correctly.

6. Long-term service and support

We stay in touch and connect you with reputable service stations, repairers and parts suppliers across Sri Lanka for long-term maintenance.
